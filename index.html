About me

Hi, my name is Ibnu and I'm a fresh graduated from Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya (PENS). I am a strategic, rationalist, realistist person and enjoy to learn a new things.

I have passion for programming robots, Electronics design and pushing the Boundaries of what can be achieved through digital.

I have studied the world of robotics since I entered the Junior High School (SMP) level. Became the head of Robotics at Public High School (SMA) 2, Surabaya. Became a finalist for the Indonesia ICT Award in 2015. In 2018, I won first place in the Maritime National Week competition held at the Faculty of Engineering, Hasanuddin University. In 2019, I began to research 3-dimensional mapping using the lidar sensor on the Quadrotor vehicle as my final project.
